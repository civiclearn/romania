Preparing for the Romanian Citizenship Interview

21/01/2025

The Romanian citizenship interview is a crucial step in the naturalization process. It is designed to assess your knowledge of the Romanian language, culture, history, and constitution. To succeed, it is essential to prepare thoroughly and understand what the interviewers expect from you.

What to Expect During the Interview

  1. Romanian Language Proficiency
    You will be required to demonstrate basic language skills. This may include reading a short text, engaging in a simple conversation, or answering questions about your personal background.
  2. Knowledge of Romanian Culture and History
    The interview will test your understanding of key historical events, cultural traditions, national symbols, and important figures. Familiarity with the Constitution of Romania and citizens’ rights is also essential.
  3. Personal Interaction
    Interviewers may ask about your motivations for becoming a Romanian citizen and your plans for the future.

How to Prepare Effectively

Preparing for the interview requires a structured approach. Here are the key areas to focus on:

  • Major historical events and dates;
  • National holidays and symbols;
  • Romanian traditions and cultural heritage;
  • Basic constitutional principles and citizens’ rights;
  • Common Romanian phrases and expressions.
